Become a Patron

If you enjoy Blokada and want to support its development, becoming a Patron is a great way to do so! Your website will be promoted to every user of Blokada.

Criteria

  • Your website cannot contain any content deemed unsuitable by the Blokada community.
  • Your website cannot be visually obtrusive, bloated or load slowly. It has to use TLS.
  • Consider the fact Blokada community does not like advertisement. You should generally only promote products and services for good cause and/or beneficial for the community.
  • You should make a donation of sufficient amount. Offering services or freebies for the project and/or the community may also be accepted instead.

How to apply?

  1. Send your application to hello@blokada.org explaining why you should be chosen as a Patron. Include the name of website you would like to promote.
  2. You will get a response promptly.
  3. If accepted, your application will be included in the next voting of the Blokada community.
  4. Blokada users vote for new Patron once a month. If you win the vote, you are in!

Where will my website be displayed?

Your website will be prominently promoted in a screen displayed once for every new user, and once after every app update, just after opening Blokada. This guarantees that every impression is having user attention.

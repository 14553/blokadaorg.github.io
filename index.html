About Blokada

Blokada is a compact app that transparently blocks unwanted content like ads, tracking, malware and other annoyances. It protects your privacy, saves your data plan and makes your device faster by preventing it from downloading and displaying unnecessary content.

Blokada works across all of your apps, not only the browser! It doesn't require root, supports both wifi and mobile networks, and is perfectly safe and simple to use. Just turn it on, and it will do the job silently!

Download Blokada

Blokada is a not allowed on Google Play because it interferes with Google's business model. To install it, you need to choose one of alternative ways. Please contact us in case you experience any problems. We will help you!

Install .apk directly (sideloading)

Please follow instructions on how to sideload apps. Note that Blokada will not be automatically updated when installed this way.

Download Blokada .apk



Install from Blokada Store

Blokada Store is our custom app marketplace powered by Aptoide. When installed from this store, Blokada will be automatically updated. Please first follow instructions on how to sideload apps.



Install from Amazon App Store

This is the recommended way if you already have Amazon App Store on your device. Blokada will be automatically updated.



Install from XDA Labs

XDA Labs is a marketplace for apps not available on Google Play, made by xda-developers.com. You can always find the newest Blokada there. Updates are installed automatically.
